SSSynthesiser.js example

How to use SSSynthesiser.js to play G.Sviridov 'Time, Forward!'.

  • Initialize [not initialized]
  • Play
  • Stop
  • Rewind
  • Mute drums | instruments | none
  • Loop
    • whole song
    • piece 1
      • 0:0
    • piece 2
      • 0:1, 1:1, 2:1, 3:1
        0:2, 1:2, 2:2, 3:2
    • piece 3
      • 0:3, 1:3, 2:3, 3:3
        0:4, 1:4, 2:4, 3:4
  • Hit drum bass | snare | hihat
  • Hit piano A | H | C | D | E | F | G | a
  • Hit chord A | Am | C | Cm
